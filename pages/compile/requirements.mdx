<Callout type="warning">
Compiling dahliaOS is a very resource-intensive process.  
A Dell Optiplex 790 with a 3.0 GHz i5-2400 CPU and 16 GB of RAM needs about 8 hours
</Callout>

### Hardware Requirements
- A 64-bit x86_64 CPU with at least 4 cores.
- At least 8 GB of RAM.
- About 64 GB of hard drive space.

### Software Requirements
- A 64-bit Linux distribution.
- Docker (see next page for installation instructions).
